Translation

Translation functions allow you to move elements left, right, up, or down. These functions are similar to the behavior of position: relative; when declaring top and left , moving elements up and down or left and right along the x and y axes. When you employ a translation function, youâ€™re moving elements without impacting the flow of the document. Unlike position: relative , which allows you to position an element either against its current position or against a parent or other ancestor, a translated element can only be moved relative to its current position.
